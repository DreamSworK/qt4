◆ SwipeDirection

enum QSwipeGesture::SwipeDirection

This enum describes the possible directions for the gesture's motion along the horizontal and vertical axes.

  • NoDirection The gesture had no motion associated with it on a particular axis.
  • Left The gesture involved a horizontal motion to the left.
  • Right The gesture involved a horizontal motion to the right.
  • Up The gesture involved an upward vertical motion.
  • Down The gesture involved a downward vertical motion.

qreal QSwipeGesture::swipeAngle

the angle of the motion associated with the gesture
